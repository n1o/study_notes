# Area and Volume

## Area of a Reactangle
A = ab

## Volume of a Box
V = abc

## Circle
A circle with radius $r$ has diameter $d = 2r$

The circumference 
$C=2\pi r$

Area:
$A = \pi r^2$

## Sphere
A sphere with radius $r$

The area:
$$
A = 4\pi r^2
$$

The volume:
$$
V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3
$$

## Cylinder
The volume of a cylinder with radius r and height h.

$V = \pi r h$
